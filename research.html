Research



Statistical Mechanics, Probability Theory, Mathematical Physics and their Applications.

The main subjects are:

  • Non-equilibrium Statistical Mechanics and Interacting Particle Systems
    (e.g. duality, inclusion process, Fourier's law).

  • Disordered Systems, namely systems on random spatial structures with random interactions
    (e.g. spin models on random graphs, spin glasses).

  • Large Deviations and Rare Events
    (e.g. importance sampling with cloning).

  • Models for evolution, economicy and social sciences
    (mathematical population genetics, portfolio optimization, stochastic processes on random graphs).
