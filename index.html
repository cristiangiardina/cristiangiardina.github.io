Cristian Giardinà is Full Professor of Mathematical Physics at Modena and Reggio Emilia University  and Visiting Professor in Probability at Delft University.

He has been Assistant Professor in Probability at Eindhoven University and Post-Doc at EURANDOM, Bologna University and Brown University.

His research interests are: Statistical Mechanics, Interacting particle systems, Probability Theory and their Applications.
